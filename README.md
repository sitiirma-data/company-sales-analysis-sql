# 📊 Company Sales Analysis (SQL Project)

## 📌 Objective

Analyze sales data from an import-export food company to uncover insights on sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.

---

## 🛠 Tools

* SQL (PostgreSQL)
* Excel

---

## 📊 Business Questions

1. What is the average delivery time?
2. When do sales peak?
3. Which shipping vendor is most efficient?
4. What are the top and worst performing products?
5. How do gross and net sales perform monthly?
6. What is the yearly sales growth?
7. Which employees contribute the most to sales?
8. How does each product category contribute yearly?

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Average delivery time is **8.49 days**, indicating potential inefficiencies in logistics
* Sales peak during **Q1 to early Q2**, especially April
* Speedy Express is the most cost-efficient shipping vendor
* A large gap exists between top-performing and low-performing products
* Sales show a seasonal trend: high at the beginning and end of the year
* Sales grew significantly (190%) from 2013 to 2014
* Sales contributions are uneven across employees
* Beverages category consistently contributes the most to sales
